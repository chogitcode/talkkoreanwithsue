제 2과 한글을 배워요 (2)

In previous chapter, we have learned 10 simple vowels.
Through this chapter, we are going to learn 11 compound vowels.

What is compound vowels? It's made of combination of simple vowels.

Let's have a look 11 compound vowels.



As you can see the picture, some vowels are pronounced similarly.
For example :

1. ㅔ(e), ㅐ(ae)
2.ㅖ(ye),ㅒ(yae)
3.ㅙ(wae), ㅚ(oe), ㅞ(wae)

Their pronunciations are slightly different. But I would say there's no difference in real conversation. It is totally OK to pronounce them the same way.

You have now learned how to pronounce compound vowels. Then, Let's have a look how to write compound vowels.



In this chapter, we learned how to read and write 11 compound vowels. We will continue to learn the consonants in the next chapter.
