제 3과 한글을 배워요 (3)

You learned all the vowels through the last chapter. Well done! ( 잘했어요! )
In this chapter, let's move on to the consonants part. As I mentioned in the 1st chapter,
there are 19 consonants in Hangul.

Hangul consonants are consists of 14 simple consonants and 5 double consonants.
All the consonant are used in combinations with the vowels. That is why we have learned all the vowels in the previous chapter. Let's start with 14 simple consonants first.



Through the picture above, you learned how to pronounce consonants.
But, you still don't know name of each consonant. Let's have a look table below.

ㄱ 기역 (giyeok)
ㄴ 니은 (nieun)
ㄷ 디귿 (digeut)
ㄹ 리을 (rieul)
ㅁ 미음 (mieum)
ㅂ 비읍 (bieup)
ㅅ 시옷 (siot)
ㅇ 이응 (ieung)
ㅈ 지읏 (jieut)
ㅊ 치읓 (chieut)
ㅋ 키읔 (kieuk)
ㅌ 티읕 (tieut)
ㅍ 피읖 (pieup)
ㅎ 히읕 (hieut)


These are 14 simple consonants. From now, we will be learning rest of consonants. Double consonants consist of 5 different consonants and they are called "tense" consonant.

Until now, we learned both consonants and vowels. In the next lesson, we will learn how to make Korean letters. 다음 시간에 만나요 (See you next lesson!)
